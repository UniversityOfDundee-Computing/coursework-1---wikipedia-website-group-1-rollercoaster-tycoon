Game Reception Page

When opening the game you are greeted by an animated background along with cheers and screams of people enjoying themselfs as well as the title of the game "Roller Coaster Tycoon" in the top right. You have four buttons along the bottom which will allow you to either start a new game, load a previously played game, do the tutorial or exit the game.
